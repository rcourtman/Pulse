## Owner Decision

The release owner ended the moving v6.1.0 RC feedback loop and directed one
stable cutoff from current `main`. The prepared `v6.1.0-rc.5` tag and GitHub
release were never created. v6.1.0 may therefore publish stable without another
RC publication or the normal 72-hour soak.

This is a one-version owner-risk acceptance, not validation evidence and not a
standing policy for later releases. The final release-preparation commit may
change only governed version, release-note, qualification, and release-control
metadata on top of the content cutoff base. The exact publish SHA must be bound
by the no-public-release `Release Dry Run` and then consumed by the normal
single-build release workflow without further product changes.

## Cutoff Reopen Rule

Only a security defect, data-loss risk, broken upgrade, startup failure, or
release-pipeline defect may reopen v6.1.0. Ordinary product findings, polish,
and non-critical feedback after the cutoff belong in v6.1.1.
